# Data Quality Summary

As part of the data collection and exploration phase, all 10 provided CSV datasets were successfully loaded using Pandas. Each dataset was examined using the `.shape`, `.dtypes`, and `.head()` functions to understand its structure, data types, and sample records.

The Fund Master dataset was explored to identify the available fund houses, categories, sub-categories, and risk grades. This helped in understanding the overall structure of the mutual fund data.

The AMFI scheme codes from the Fund Master dataset were validated against the NAV History dataset. The validation confirmed that every scheme in the Fund Master dataset had a corresponding NAV history, indicating that the datasets were consistent and could be linked without any missing scheme codes.

Live NAV data was successfully fetched from the MFAPI for the specified mutual fund scheme and saved as a CSV file. NAV data for the five required mutual fund schemes was also retrieved successfully.

Overall, the datasets were found to be well-structured and suitable for further analysis. No major issues affecting data usability were observed during the initial exploration, providing a reliable foundation for the next stages of the project.
